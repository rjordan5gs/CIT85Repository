The Golden Age

Guts and The Band of the Hawk (General Overview)

Mercenary Guts, infamously known as The Black Swordsman,
is defeated in combat by the skilled and charismatic Griffith.
Afterwards, he's forced to join Griffith's group, The Band of the Hawk,
and undertake many dangerous missions that soon turn bizarre.

Reasons why you should read the manga:

The characters in Berserk are, hands down, well made. The time and effort
in building all these characters can be seen on the elaborate development
that the creator pushed in the entire story. Each of these characters are made
in such a way that the reader will be able to understand and invest emotions in.

Warning! Berserk is rated M for Mature!

In terms of genre and theme, Berserk is classified as a dark fantasy, medieval era,
supernatural, seinen manga. It is intended and made for mature readers due to
its heavy gore and sexual depictions

Should a 13 year old watch Berserk? The answer is a resounding NO.
The work of Miura is made for adults, being part of the seinen demographic,
which lots of gore and incredibly graphic content.

Author:

Kentaro Miura (三浦 建太郎, Miura Kentarō, July 11, 1966 – May 6, 2021) was a Japanese manga artist.
He was best known for his acclaimed dark fantasy series Berserk, which began serialization in 1989 and
continued until his death. As of 2021, Berserk had more than 50 million copies in circulation, making it
one of the best-selling manga series of all time. In 2002, Miura received the Award for Excellence at the
6th Tezuka Osamu Cultural Prizes.
